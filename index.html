Welcome to Yum Juices , industry-leading manufacturers of quality juices, smoothies and fruit/confectionary sauces to the retail and food service sectors

Yum Juices is a young company that serves up delicious, nutritious, energising smoothies and juices. The comp any has a large portfolio of products ranging from dairy - free, gluten - free, vegetarian and vegan smoothies and juices. Additionally, Yum Juices provides, to their consumers, details of the main ingredients of their products to ensure that those allergic to certain food don’t absorb the wrong type of food.
